About Me

I am a computer science & engineering student at MiraCosta College working towards transferring to a four-year institution. I have a strong background in mathematics and the fine arts, so I strive to create a world in which I can utilize both. I currently serve as a mathematics and computer science tutor, a supplemental instructional leader for precalculus, and the president of WiCS (Women in Computer Science).
